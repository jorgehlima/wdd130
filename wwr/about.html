Rafting Travel Co.

Our mission is to provide thrilling, safe, and memorable white-water rafting adventures for individuals, families, and groups. Join us on the river and experience the adventure of a lifetime!

History

Rafting Travel Co. began its journey in 1998, when founder and lifelong river guide Mark Delaney organized his first series of professional rafting tours along the Snake River in Wyoming. His goal was simple: to create safe, memorable, and environmentally responsible river adventures for families and explorers of all ages. By 2003, growing demand allowed the company to expand its operations to Idaho’s Salmon River and Colorado’s Arkansas River, establishing Rafting Travel Co. as a trusted name in the outdoor adventure industry.

In 2008, the company introduced specialized youth programs and wilderness training courses, attracting schools and community groups.A major milestone came in 2010, when Rafting Travel Co. launched its first international expeditions to Costa Rica and Peru, opening the door to tropical white-water experiences. The company continued its upward momentum and, in 2016, opened its flagship Adventure Headquarters, complete with training facilities, gear shops, and a conservation education center. Today, Rafting Travel Co. remains committed to safety, sustainability, and creating unforgettable river memories for adventurers around the world.
