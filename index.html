Wilmington - NC

Wilmington, NC, is a historic port city on the Cape Fear River known for its beaches, mild climate, and as a major filming location, earning it the nickname "Hollywood of the East". It has a rich history dating back to the colonial era, and today features attractions like the Battleship USS North Carolina, the Riverwalk, and various museums and historic homes. The area is also known for its outdoor recreation, including parks and nearby beaches like Wrightsville and Carolina Beach.
