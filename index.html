WDD 130 | Jorge Henrique de Souza Lima

Hi! My name is Jorge, and I'm originally from Belém in the state of Pará, located in Brazil's Amazon region. I served a mission in the Brazil Belo Horizonte Mission from February 1989 to February 1991. In February 1993, at age 23, I moved to the United States with my parents and sibling. I love to read, hunt, and fish. I'm also passionate about all things medieval. J.R.R. Tolkien is my favorite writer—I own many of his books and have read them several times. My wife and I currently live in Wilmington, NC.

North Carolina

North Carolina is a U.S. state in the Southeast with diverse geography ranging from the Appalachian Mountains in the west to the Atlantic coast in the east, separated by the central Piedmont region. It is one of the original 13 colonies, with a rich history and a diverse culture, and its capital is Raleigh, while Charlotte is its most populous city.
