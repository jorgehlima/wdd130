North Carolina

North Carolina is a U.S. state in the Southeast with diverse geography ranging from the Appalachian Mountains in the west to the Atlantic coast in the east, separated by the central Piedmont region. It is one of the original 13 colonies, with a rich history and a diverse culture, and its capital is Raleigh, while Charlotte is its most populous city.
